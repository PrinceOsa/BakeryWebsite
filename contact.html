Our Story

"Love to hear from you"

Located: Salisbury, NC

Phone: 704-333-3333

Rhondas Delights can't wait to talk to you!

It all started with my love for baking. Since a child I was always fascinated with the many pastries my Grandmother would make every weekend. When I was old enough to help I soaked in as much as I could learn the delicate techniques she would use to make the most flavourful desserts I've ever tasted. I took those same recipes and techniques and created Rhonda's Delicious Delights. Sharing with the word the same flavors that completely entranced me with every bite.
